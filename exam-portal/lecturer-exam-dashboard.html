Upload Word Document

üìã Document Format Guide

Format your questions as follows:

1. What is the capital of Ghana? A. Kumasi B. Accra C. Tamale D. Takoradi Answer: B 2. Is Ghana in West Africa? A) True B) False Answer: A OR you can also write: 2. Is Ghana in West Africa? Answer: True (Will automatically become: A) True, B) False) 3. Explain the importance of communication in the military. Answer: [Your expected answer here]

‚úÖ IMPORTANT: Correct answers are automatically extracted from "Answer:" lines. You don't need to set them manually in the system!
Format: Use "Answer: B" for multiple choice (letter), "Answer: True" for true/false, or "Answer: [text]" for short answer/essay.

Tips: Use numbered questions (1, 2, 3...), mark options with A, B, C, D, and always include "Answer:" followed by the correct answer. For multiple choice, options should be on separate lines.

Only .docx files are supported (not .doc). You can save older .doc files as .docx in Word.
